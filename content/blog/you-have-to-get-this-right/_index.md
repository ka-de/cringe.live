I got really good at making portraits of subjects, I have a completely different frustration now, though&#x2025;

There is a big event in the small city I live in currently and as I'm cooped up in my two bedroom apartment alone,
generating AI art for a contest I shouldn't even be participating in normally. But someone told me a month ago that
I'm not normal, so with the help of many doctors who treat every situation like a depression, I'm now on a ride to
find the correct vocabulary to describe a cybernetically enhanced mushroom in the background.

As the people under my open windows are enjoying their purchased goods, I can hear children screaming in the distance,
and can't help but think, they are looking at some horrible AI art with the eyes all wrong&#x2025;

> "You have to get this right!"

I'm not sure if I have to get anything right, I mean, I'm just going to load up the best checkpoint I could find
that someone else made and use the best possible negative prompts someone else made and use the best possible
prompt I can come up with my deranged mind just like someone else and that is exactly why you have to vote for my
entry, bitch!

Well, World, please welcome my creation, Terence Mckenna, my hero, a person who did too many drugs, and as a person
who almost did too many drugs, I could think of no better person, possibly because I'm deranged, to Cyberpunkify.
